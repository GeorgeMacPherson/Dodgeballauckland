How it works

Whether you’ve played before or you’re brand new, joining a league is simple.

Step 1

Choose your league

Pick a night and location that works for you from our social, womens and youth options.

Step 2

Register online

Complete the quick Google Form – join with friends as a team or sign up solo and we’ll place you.

Step 3

Show up & play

Turn up on game night, meet your team, warm up and get throwing. Music, good vibes, high-energy fun.

Register to play

Use the form below to register as a team or individual. We’ll confirm your league placement, fees and start date by email.

About Dodgeball Auckland
